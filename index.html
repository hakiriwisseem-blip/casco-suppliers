About Our Company

Casco Products is part of the Amphenol Corporation. As a multi-billion-dollar Fortune 500 company, Amphenol is one of the largest manufacturers of interconnect products in the world.

Founded in 1932, Amphenol designs, manufactures and markets electrical, electronic and fiber optic connectors, interconnect systems, antennas, sensors and sensor-based products and coaxial and high-speed specialty cable.

The primary end markets include communications and information processing, cable television, cellular telephone, data communication systems, aerospace and military electronics, and automotive, rail and other transportation and industrial applications.
